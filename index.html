Our Story

Started on March 15, 2022, SM Tiffin Services carries the legacy of two great friends - Shakti sinh and Mangal sinh, whose initials form our brand name.

It all began at Kumudwadi Raj Chamunda tea post, where my friend Amar sinh suggested I start a tiffin service. Though hesitant at first, with his persistent encouragement, I took the leap. Amar sinh became our first customer, followed by Kishor bhai, and through word of mouth, we quickly grew into a trusted name in Bhavnagar.
